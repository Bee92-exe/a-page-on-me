My Shayari

Urdu/Hindi poetry written in English letters
Because emotions don't need a script

A Note About This Collection

I can speak and understand Urdu/Hindi but can't write in the script. So I write the way it sounds - in English alphabet. This is my way of keeping the soul of shayari alive, unbound by scripts. Each line is written as I would speak it, preserving the original emotion and rhythm.

Dil Ki Kami

Dil ki galiyon mein teri kami ka andhera chhaya hai, Har saans mein tera intezaar zinda hai… Tujhe bhool kar bhi tere khwabon se milta rehta hoon, Aaj raat bhi mein teri yaadon ka zehar pee raha hoon…

Khwab Aur Haqeeqat

Tere bina har khwab adhoora sa lagta hai, Jaise kitaab mein sirf khaali safhe bache hon. Tum aao to lagta hai sab kuch poora hai, Tum jaao to lagta hai zindagi hi khatam hai.

Chup Ki Zubaan

Khamoshi meri zubaan hai, jo kuch nahi kehti, Magar har lafz mein teri dastaan kehti hai. Log sunte hain to awaaz nahi aati, Magar mere dil ki har dhadkan tera naam kehti hai.

Waqt Ki Raftaar

Waqt guzar gaya, magar yaadein nahi guzri, Tum door chale gaye, magar khushboo nahi gayi. Aaj bhi wohi lamhe, wohi baatein, wohi hasi, Zindagi badal gayi, magar mohabbat nahi badli.

Ek Ajeeb Dard

Ek ajeeb sa dard hai jo chhupa kar rakha hai, Na dikhata hai, na batata hai, bas jeene nahi deta. Koi pooche to keh deta hoon sab theek hai, Magar andar se toot raha hoon har pal.

Ishq Ya Ibadat

Mere ishq ko dekh kar khuda bhi ghabra gaya, Samjha yeh bhi koi bandagi ka sauda hai... Par afsos... tune kab samjha, Ke meri mohabbat toh aaj bhi tere naam ki ibadat hai.

Tanhai Ka Sath

Tanhai ne sikhaya hai mujhe khud se baat karna, Khud ko samjhana, khud ko rulana, khud ko hasana. Log kehte hain akela hai, magar sach to ye hai, Mein khud hi apna sabse acha dost ban gaya hoon.

Meri Mohabbat

Meri mohabbat ek aisi kitaab hai, Jise sirf mein hi padh sakta hoon. Tumne shuruwat ki likhne ki koshish ki, Magar khatam karna meri majboori hai.

Raaton Ki Lambai

Raatein lambi ho gayi hain tere jaane ke baad, Har pal ek zindagi barabar lagta hai. Sote waqt bhi jaagta rehta hoon, Kahin tum khwabon mein aa jao, na chhoot jao.

Zakhm Aur Marham

Tumne zakhm diye the, mein ne marham banaya, Tumne toot kar diya, mein ne jod kar rakha. Aaj bhi wohi dard hai, wohi yaadein hain, Magar ab shikayat karne wala koi nahi.

Meri Kahani

Meri kahani mein hero bhi mein hoon, Aur villain bhi mein hoon. Kisi aur ki zaroorat hi nahi, Khud hi sab kuch kar leta hoon.

Zindegi Ek Imtihaan

Na khuda samjha, na tum... Bas main hi jalta raha apne jazbaat ki aag mein, Tere bina jeena bhi kya jeena, Maut bhi lagti hai zindagi mere ek imtihaan hai.

Na Wo Mila Na Khuda

Maine apni saari mohabbat usko de di,
Khuda ke liye kya bachata? Dil ke saare diye usi ke naam kar diye,
Khuda ke huzoor kya jalata? Woh chali gayi to dil bhi khaali ho gaya,
Khuda ko bhi kho baitha saath mein. Na mehboob mila, na Khuda ka aasra raha,
Main khud ko bhi kahin chor aaya tha raste mein.

Khud Se Anjaan Musafir

Mohabbat ke safar mein sab kuch luta diya, Ab toh sirf ek khud se anjaan musafir hoon main, Jise na Khuda mila, na uska reh gaya jo Khuda ke the.

Why I Write This Way

Roman Urdu/Hindi is how I preserve the sounds and emotions of the language I love but cannot write in its script. Each word is written exactly as it sounds when spoken. This style keeps the poetry authentic and accessible.

When you read these lines, read them aloud. You'll hear the music of Urdu/Hindi in English letters. The emotion, the rhythm, the pain - it's all there, just in a different dress.
